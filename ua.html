Software Development at Under Armour


In the Summer of 2017 I had the opportunity to intern at Under Armour's Global Headquarters located in Baltimore, Maryland. I was one of 98 out of 14,000+ who applied for the Rookie Summer League, UA's internship program. For 12 weeks I lived with three other interns in the Fells Point neighborhood of Baltimore and worked around 40 hours per week.

I was in the IT Retail department and worked on the Under Armour Center of Retail Excellence, an SAP Fiori iOS application.I designed and created the Bin Location experience to enable store associates to digitally inventory and replenish stock. I also competed in and won an internal IT project competition by developing a Cloud First strategy. Most of my work was done on SAP's Web IDE plaftorm using Javascript, HTML, XML, and CSS, but there was also a fair amount of back end work done using SQL and SAP's own libraries.

This internship experience was amazing because I was able to work independently on an impactful project for a large global company while also having tons of fun on the weekends. Within my team at Under Armour, I was the only developer on my team which made my work more independent in nature. I had to pick up SAP-specific libraries and concepts quickly and the majority of work was learned on-the-job. I was grateful to be near Washington D.C., my hometown, because on the weekends I was able to visit my friends.
