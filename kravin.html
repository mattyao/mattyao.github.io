Software Engineering at Kravin

Kravin is a platform built to create experiences between you, your friends, and the people who share your cravings. You can explore products, events, and services available around you and select what you crave, or join in on what your friends crave. Then, you can act on those cravings with a group of your Kravin friends by organizing an event through the app.

I interned at Kravin in Downtown Berkeley during Summmer 2016. At the time I joined the company, the app was still undergoing the second half of its initial development so the team needed better testing and maintenance of the app's backend structure. I implemented AWS Lambda functions to automate management and aggregate data for a MySQL database. I also conducted user tests and contributed to design mockups of features.
