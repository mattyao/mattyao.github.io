User Experience at Vidy

Vidy innovates on the bleeding edge of social media. They are partnered with the world's biggest celebrities and tech companies building unique social experiences that push mobile and communication to their limits.

I joined Vidy during Spring 2017 to work on the design team as a User Experience Researcher. I commuted to the San Francisco office about once a week to present my findings and synthesize with the rest of the design team. During the rest of the week I conducted user tests, organized and analyzed user data and designed key functionalities for the mobile app. Most of my interaction was with the CTO and the engineering team to help them prioritize key features and the product roadmap of the company.
